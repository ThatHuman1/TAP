TAP is a pilot program in Nova Scotia that stems off of P-TECH. TAP stands for Technology Advantage Program, it’s goal is to teach students about technology and get a degree in a field of Technology. It’s a 6 year program which starts in grade 9 and continues through to college where students get two years of there college paid for by IBM.
